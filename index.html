ABOUT ECS Entrepreneurs

We aim to build a platform for students who share same entrepreneurial inspiration and aspiration on starting their business. Our society organises regular talks, workshops and competitions to offer essential knowledge and skills to these young talents and provides precious opportunities to connect people together.

Seminars

Topics cover entrepreneurial stories, information on starting a company, technology trends. We are sponsored by ... so expect some big names!

Workshops

Workshops include technical skills such as coding skills and soft skills like business model development and team management.

Competitions

Competitions will grant you valuable experience and ideas...of course, we will give prizes to the winners!
